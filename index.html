Benefits of learning coding

There are number of reasons as to why we should learn coding....

  1. You'll be able to fine-tune your site's blogposts or even your whole theme
  2. You'd have the options of using a faster & safer 'static CMS'
  3. You can build your own landing pages
  4. You can rummage through other sites' code to see how it all works
  5. You could work more effectively with digital designers and developers
  6. You'll have more confidence, and might end up in an entirely new career
